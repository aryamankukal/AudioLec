Our Mission

Our simple and efficient service helps students document class
lectures expand their knowledge of the subjects they're learning by
recommending them relavent and enlightening videos based on the
their teacher's lessons. Using Speech Recognition and Natural
Language Processing, we are paving the path towards an education
system in which every student has the resources to learn above
and beyond.

Record

Use built-in speech recognition technology to record speech and get the audio transcribed.

View

View an analysis report which includes a transcription, entity analysis, and smart video recommendations based on the lesson.

Send

Have the report emailed to yourself for quick reference or added into a Google Doc for safer keeping.
